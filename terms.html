Mipango Mchezo App Terms and Conditions


1. Definitions


• Circles: Groups of users who contribute funds and receive payouts in a rotating manner.
• Admin: The entity responsible for managing and regulating circles.
• Digital Merry-Go-Round: The platform that facilitates financial contributions and payouts.
• Contributions: The payments made by users into their respective circles.
• Dashboard: The user interface that displays circle details, contributions, and payouts.
• Payouts: Funds distributed to eligible circle members upon completion of a cycle.

2. User Participation


Users can join circles, make contributions, and receive payouts by registering and fulfilling the platform’s requirements.

3. Eligibility


Users must be at least 18 years old to participate.

4. Participant Roles


Users must:
• Pay contributions diligently for their selected circle.
• Provide a referee for accountability.
• Complete NIDA verification.
• Provide account details for receiving payouts.

5. Platform Commitment


• Mipango activates bot players to ensure circles with at least one member start and complete on time.
• Ensures all fully contributing participants receive payouts in full and on time.

6. Contribution Deadlines and Penalties


Mipango may penalize users missing deadlines as follows:
• 1-hour delay: 2% penalty of the contribution amount.
• 1-day delay: Full contribution amount penalty.
• 2-day delay: Two full contributions as a penalty.
• 3-day delay: Three full contributions as a penalty.
• 4-day delay: User forfeits all prior contributions.

7. Account Management


• Contributions can be made via mobile money or bank transfers.
• Contribution amounts range from TSh 1,500 to TSh 1,000,000.
• Payouts are automatically deposited into the recipient’s registered account.

8. Fund Handling


• Funds are pooled in a CRDB and Mipango partnership account.
• Neither party can withdraw user funds.
• Only admin fees, penalties, and interest gains can be withdrawn under agreed terms.
• Payout frequency varies (daily, every 3 days, 10 days, weekly, or monthly).
• Users can change their receiving account, subject to a service charge.

9. App Security


User accounts are secured by PIN, Face ID, or Fingerprint authentication, activated during onboarding and adjustable via the profile page. User will be solely responsible for the safe keeping and the confidentiality of Password/Access code and such other item relevant or pertaining to the Account, and do utmost efforts to prevent the loss of and /or use of the same by any third party. User shall keep Mipango informed immediately upon becoming aware any of the Password/Access Codes is compromised.

10. Debt Recovery Measures


If a user fails to contribute after receiving a payout, Mipango may:
• Send in-app reminders.
• Contact the user and their referees.
• Auto-deduct funds from the user’s mobile money or bank account.
• Deduct from guarantors’ accounts if feasible.
• Engage independent debt collectors for legal recovery.

11. Loan Assistance


Mipango may issue loans to active users to prevent contribution delays.
• A 10% interest fee shall apply, automatically deducted from the user's payout.

12. Deductions and Charges


Mipango may deduct user payouts for:
• Penalties
• Admin fees
• Loans issued (principal, interest, and processing fees)
Users will receive full breakdowns of all deductions before payout.

13. Platform Compliance


Users must refrain from fraudulent activities and adhere to platform rules.

14. Consequences of Breach


• Suspension for repeated contribution delays.
• Permanent ban for serious infractions.

15. Fees and Charges

• Administration fee: 2% of the total circle contribution.
• Late payment penalties (outlined in section 6).

16. Risk Disclosure


• No risk in receiving payouts.
• Potential payout delays due to payment system issues.
• Risk of monetary loss due to unforeseen fraud.

17. Insurance



• Users are charged an insurance fee as part of administrative costs.

18. Investment Risks


Funds may be invested by partner banks, posing potential liquidity risks.

19. Data Collection and Privacy
• Mipango collects personal data (name, DOB, gender, region, income, phone number, and account details) for verification and transaction processing.
• Data is encrypted for security.

20. Data Sharing Policy


Mipango may share user data:
• With third parties for marketing and service improvement.
• With the Central Bank of Tanzania for compliance.
• With government entities when legally required.

21. Legal Compliance



Mipango adheres to Tanzania’s data protection laws.

22. Dispute Resolution



Disputes will be resolved via:
1. Direct engagement with Mipango customer support.
2. Escalation to Mipango officials.
3. Mediation at the Tanzania Department for Mediation.
4. Court of law if unresolved.
